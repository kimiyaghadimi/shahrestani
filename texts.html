    Title

    • Pahlavi: Šahrestānīhā ī Ērān šahr
    • English: The Provincial Capitals of Ērān-šahr

    Introduction:

  • Line 1
    • Pahlavi: pad nām ī dādār ī weh abzōnīg
    • English: In the name of the beneficient bountiful Creator
  • Line 2
    • Pahlavi: pad nām ud nērōg ud ayārīh ī dādār Ohrmazd ǰadag ī nēk.
    • English: In the name and power and righteousness of the Creator Ohrmazd and good omen.

    • Body:

  • Sentence 1
    • Pahlavi: šahrestānīhā ī andar zamīg ī Ērān-šahr kerd estēd ǰud ǰud būm kū kadām sar-xwadāy kerd pad gōkān abar ēn ayādgār nibišt estēd.
    • English: The cities in the land of Ērān-šahr which have been built in different days, where and which overlord made them is written in detail in this memoir.
  • Sentence 2
    • Pahlavi: pad kust ī xwarāsān samarkand šahrestān kay-us ī kawādān bun fragand siyāwaxš ī kay-usān bē frazāmēnīd.
    • English: In the Eastern direction, the foundation of the city of Samarkand was laid by Kāus, the son of Kawād. Sīyāwaxš, the son of Kāus completed it.
  • Sentence 3
    • Pahlavi: kay-husrō ī siyāwaxšān ānōh zād u-š warzāwand ātaxš wahrām ānōh nišāst.
    • English: Kay Husraw, the son of Sīyāwaxš was born there , and he set the miraculous Wahrām fire there.
  • Sentence 4

    • English: Then Zoroaster brought the Religion. By the order of king Wištāsp 1200 fragard (chapters) in the script of religious scripture were engraved on golden tablets and written and deposited in the treasury of that fire-(temple).

    • English: Sogdiana (has) seven abodes, its having seven abodes in it means that there were seven Lords in it. One belonging to Jam and one to Aži Dahāg and one to Frēdōn and one to Manūčihr and one to Kāus and one to Lohrāsp and one to king Wištāsp.
