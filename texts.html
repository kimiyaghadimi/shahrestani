By the order of king Wištāsp 1200 fragard (chapters) in the script of religious scripture were engraved on golden tablets and written and deposited in the treasury of that fire-(temple).

One belonging to Jam and one to Aži Dahāg and one to Frēdōn and one to Manūčihr and one to Kāus and one to Lohrāsp and one to king Wištāsp.

  • 8 andar baxl ī bāmīg šahrestānī nawāzag spandyād ī wištāspān kerd. In the brilliant Balx, the city of Nawāzag was built by Spandyād, the son of Wištāsp.

And he set the miraculous Wahrām fire there and struck his lance there and he sent a message to Yabbu Xāgān, Sinjēbik Xāgān, and Čōl Xāgān and the Great Xāgān and Gohram and Tuzāb and Arzāsp, the king of the Hayōns: “behold my lance, whoever beholds the movement of this lance is like they have rushed to Iran.”

  • 14 šahrestānī tūs tūs ī nōdarān [kerd ī] nōh-sad sāl spāhbed būd. pas az tūs spāhbedīh ō zarēr ud az zarēr ō bastwar ud az bastwar ō karazm mad. The city of Tūs was built by Tūs, the son of Nōdar, and (he) was the general for 900 years. After Tūs, the office of general came to Zarēr and from Zarēr to Bastūr and from Bastūr to Karzam.
  • 15 šahrestān ī nēw-šāpuhr šāpuhr ī ardašīrān kerd pad ān gāh ka-š pāhlēzag ī tūr ōzad u-š pad ham gyāg šahrestān-ē framūd kerdan. The city of Nēwšābuhr was built by Šābuhr the son of Ardaxšīr. At that time when he killed the Tūrānian Pahlīzag, he ordered to build the city in the same place.

The city of Kūmis of five towers Aži Dahāg made it his own harem. The abode of the *Parthians was there. In the reign of Yazdgird, the son of Šābuhr made it during the invasion of the Čōl, at the boundary of the Gruznian Guard.

Five cities were built by Husraw, the son of Kawād: Husraw-šād, and Husraw ī Mūst-Ābād, and Wisp-šād-Husraw and Hū-bōy-Husraw, and Šād-farrox-Husraw and named them.

And he again founded the Karkōy Fire, and Ardaxšīr, the son of Pābag finished the city.
