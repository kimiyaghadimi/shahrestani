Šahrestānīhā ī Ērān šahr

This project explores a Pahlavi text alongside a Qajar-era text.

Research Question

How does the Šahrestānīhā ī Ērān šahr conceptualize its geographical imagination of Iran, and how does that conception compare to later Qajar-era understandings?
